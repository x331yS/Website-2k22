    As the world aimed to conquer Mars, Elon Musk approached the goal by 2022. Mars seemed to carry... but its work was not successful.     Alors que le monde visait la conquête de Mars, Elon Musk s’approchait du but en 2022. Mars semblait à porter .. mais ses travaux n’ont pas abouti. Malgré les technologies mises en place et les efforts fournis, il était impossible à l'humanité de fouler le sol de Mars. C’est alors qu’un groupe d’ingénieurs motivés, talentueux et ayant soif de découvertes, ont développé les technologies permettant d’accéder à la planète Mars.     Mars opens its doors to you was their slogan, and Aris was born. Derived from the name of the Greek god “Ares”, the journey to Mars was now possible for humanity. Aris allows even more. Today a powerful company, Aris is implementing a strategy of development of tourism on the red planet. Humanity is thirsty for discovery, and Aris will satisfy this craving. Their strategy? Allow everyone to be able to explore Mars, discover and set foot on the ground of a new planet, to feel the difference of setting foot on another star.
